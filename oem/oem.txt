For OEM Distribution.

The OEM.sh file is meant for developer and distribution use (or anyone else who does not need or want menu options and wishes to perform a silent install). 

How to use: 

1) Open OEM.sh with your editor of choice.
2) Uncomment what you wish to use.
3) Save.
4) Run the file (when or where needed)
5) You're done.

As always, happy browsing!
